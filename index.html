About Me

I'm a senior at Chantilly High School with a deep interest in the intersections of technology and problem-solving. I'm an organizer for TillyHacks, a student-led hackathon dedicated to fostering innovation in our community.

When I'm not coding or organizing events, you can find me diving into complex problems in competitive math and programming, exploring cybersecurity challenges in CTFs, or building robots as part of my school's FRC team, Chantilly Robotics. To unwind, I enjoy listening to music and staying active at the gym.
